Oswin Blue creative director Alex Taves started his career assisting surrealist artist / video artist Oli Goldsmith. A few years later became the art director for Joseph Mimran & Associates for his venture Joe Fresh, a clothing line. Which a few years later led him to start a gallery, Oswald’s Gallery & Goods - landing him a plug with the NY Times within months of opening. Oswin was born out of Oswald’s.

Oswin Blue is an unformed idea. The goals are not clear and Alex is often asked what its services are.

As unstable and conflicted as this era is, the answer feels just as unclear to Alex other than a firm momentum of moving forward, not just personally but collectively. This attitude landed Alex in “hot water” more times than he wishes to admit but never has he felt more validated. If it doesn’t feel forward, it’s not worth pursuing. He is obsessed with the place that art and life intersect.

Alex relocated to Sydney, Australia but toggles Toronto as his home base.
